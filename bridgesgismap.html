GIS MAPS

Navigating the Map: Zoom in on the map to display the location icons (circles). Green, yellow and reddish circles (with numbers) are location clusters and indicate the number of points in the vicinity for which data is available. Either click on the location clusters or zoom in further to decluster. Click on the location icon (circle) to display the details of bridge and other attribute information for each bridge. Alternatively, search field can be used to locate the bridge of interest.
